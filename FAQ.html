Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a car?

You can browse our available cars on the Cars page and click "Book a Ride Now" to start your reservation.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You need a valid driver’s license and a credit/debit card for payment.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the rental start time through your account dashboard.

Are there any age restrictions?

Renters must be at least 21 years old with a valid driver’s license.

Is insurance included?

Basic insurance is included in all rentals. Additional coverage options are available at checkout.
